Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026  ·  Flint Finance

Flint Finance ("Flint", "we", "our") is a personal finance tracking app for families and couples. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and password hash (stored securely by Supabase Auth).
  • Financial data you enter: Transactions, bill reminders, recurring expenses, savings vaults, loan balances, and notes. This data is stored in our database and linked to your account.
  • App settings: Budget cycle, income, category preferences, and notification settings.
  • Usage data: We do not use third-party analytics trackers. No advertising SDKs are included.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality (budget tracking, bill reminders, AI insights).
  • To sync your data across devices and with a shared ledger partner if you choose to share.
  • To generate AI-powered spending insights using Anthropic's Claude API. Only your spending totals and categories are sent — no names, emails, or account numbers.
  • To show cryptocurrency market data via CoinGecko's public API (no personal data is sent).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Anthropic — receives anonymized spending summaries to generate AI insights. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Your ledger partner — if you join a shared ledger, your transactions are visible to your partner.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete all your data at any time from the Profile screen inside the app.

5. Your Rights (Account Deletion)

You have the right to request deletion of all your personal data. To delete your account and all associated data:

  • Open Flint Finance and go to Profile → Account → Delete My Account & Data.
  • Confirm the deletion. All your transactions, settings, bills, notes, and account credentials will be permanently removed.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Row-Level Security (RLS) on our database, HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, and secure environment variable management for API keys. No sensitive credentials are stored in client-side code.

7. Children's Privacy

Flint Finance is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please contact us through the app or reach out via the GitHub repository: github.com/NotoriousKid234/Family-Budget.
